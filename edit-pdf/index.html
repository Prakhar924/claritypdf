How it works

  1. Add your PDF, then double-click the page you want to edit (or hover and click the ✎ button).
  2. Use the tools: Text to type anywhere, Draw for signatures, Highlight for passages, Whiteout to cover content.
  3. Click Done, then Download PDF. Your edits are baked permanently into the saved file.

Frequently asked questions

Can I change text that's already in the PDF?

You can cover existing text with the Whiteout tool and type replacement text over it. Editing the PDF's original text in place, like Adobe Acrobat Pro, isn't possible in a browser tool.

Can I sign a PDF with this?

Yes — use the Draw tool to sign with your mouse, trackpad, or finger on a touchscreen, then download the signed document.

Are edited files uploaded anywhere?

No. Editing, rendering, and saving all happen inside your browser.

Private by design

Most online PDF sites upload your document to their servers to process it. ClarityPDF works differently: the entire tool runs as JavaScript inside your browser, so your files are read, processed, and saved on your own device. Nothing is transmitted, stored, or logged — which makes it a safe choice for contracts, medical records, ID documents, and anything else you'd rather keep to yourself.
