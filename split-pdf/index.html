How it works

  1. Add your PDF. Every page shows up as a thumbnail you can click to select.
  2. Select the pages you want to keep — click each page, or use Select all and deselect the rest.
  3. Click Extract selected to download just those pages as a new PDF.

Frequently asked questions

Can I split one PDF into several files?

Yes — extract each group of pages in turn. Select the first set, extract, then select the next set and extract again.

Does extracting reduce quality?

No. Pages are copied directly from the original file with no re-compression, so text stays sharp and selectable.

Is it really free?

Yes — free with no account, no quotas, and no watermarks.

Private by design

Most online PDF sites upload your document to their servers to process it. ClarityPDF works differently: the entire tool runs as JavaScript inside your browser, so your files are read, processed, and saved on your own device. Nothing is transmitted, stored, or logged — which makes it a safe choice for contracts, medical records, ID documents, and anything else you'd rather keep to yourself.
