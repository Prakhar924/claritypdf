How it works

  1. Add your PDF files — drag and drop them together or choose them one by one. Every page appears as a thumbnail.
  2. Arrange the order. Use the arrow buttons on any page to move it, or delete pages you don't need.
  3. Click Download PDF. Your merged document is assembled locally and saved straight to your device.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a limit on how many PDFs I can merge?

No. There are no file limits, page caps, or daily quotas. Very large documents are only limited by your device's memory.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, which makes ClarityPDF suitable for confidential documents.

Will the merged PDF have a watermark?

Never. The output is a clean PDF with your pages exactly as you arranged them.

Private by design

Most online PDF sites upload your document to their servers to process it. ClarityPDF works differently: the entire tool runs as JavaScript inside your browser, so your files are read, processed, and saved on your own device. Nothing is transmitted, stored, or logged — which makes it a safe choice for contracts, medical records, ID documents, and anything else you'd rather keep to yourself.
