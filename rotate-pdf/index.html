How it works

  1. Add your PDF. Each page appears as a thumbnail.
  2. Select the pages that are the wrong way round — or Select all — and click Rotate 90°. Click again for 180° or 270°.
  3. Click Download PDF to save the document with rotations applied permanently.

Frequently asked questions

Can I rotate just one page?

Yes. Selection is per-page, so you can rotate a single sideways scan while leaving the rest untouched.

Is the rotation permanent?

Yes — the rotation is written into the downloaded PDF, so it opens correctly in every viewer.

Do I need to install anything?

No. It works in any modern browser on desktop or mobile.

Private by design

Most online PDF sites upload your document to their servers to process it. ClarityPDF works differently: the entire tool runs as JavaScript inside your browser, so your files are read, processed, and saved on your own device. Nothing is transmitted, stored, or logged — which makes it a safe choice for contracts, medical records, ID documents, and anything else you'd rather keep to yourself.
